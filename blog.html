Understanding Business Valuation Methods: A Comprehensive Guide

Discover the essential methods for determining your company's worth through three primary valuation approaches. The asset-based method evaluates tangible and intangible assets minus liabilities, while market-based valuation compares your business to similar companies in the industry. The income-based approach focuses on future earning potential through discounted cash flow analysis and growth projections. Choosing the right method depends on factors like business type, size, and valuation purpose. While challenges include limited market data and economic volatility, following best practices such as using multiple methods and seeking professional expertise ensures accurate valuations.
